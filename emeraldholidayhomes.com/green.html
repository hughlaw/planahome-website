The Shore Green

1 Luxury 4 Bed Apartment, 1 Luxury 3.5 Bed Apartment & 1 Luxury 2 Bed Ground Floor Apartment

Shore Green apartments offer guests two, three and four bedroom shore front apartments with balconies and panoramic views from all apartments including Ground Floor. Specifications from furnishings, lighting to layout create a tranquil ambiance, perfect for a relaxing retreat. These apartments have the added luxury of saunas, steam shower and plasma TV's. All apartments are wheelchair accessible and include full fire and security technology.
