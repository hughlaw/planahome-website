Activities & Events

Moville & Greencastle are both lively little towns, with lots to do, great places to eat, drink and enjoy live and disco music.

Local (5 mile radius)

  • Picturesque promenade walks by the water from Moville to Greencastle

  • Outdoor amusements for children and adolescents

  • Leisureland Indoor Amusement Centre (4 miles away)

  • Lots of pubs and craic

  • Affordable excellent quality restaurants

  • Greencastle-Magilligan car ferry

  • The Maritime Museum (Greencastle)

  • Angling/Fishing

Golf Clubs

  • Greencastle Golf course (0.5 mile & 3 mile)

  • Redcastle Golf Course (6 mile & 4 mile)

  • Ballyliffin 36 hole Course (16 mile)

  • Foyle Golf Course 18 Hole Course & par 3 Course (17 Miles)

  • N.I. courses convenient from ferry terminal

  • Moville Festival & Regatta

  • Greencastle Regatta

  • Inishowen Vintage Shows

  • Dylan & Beatles Fests

  • Castle Inn, Greencastle
