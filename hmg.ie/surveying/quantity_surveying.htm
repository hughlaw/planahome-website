Quantity Surveying

HMG Associates by incorporating a Quantity Surveying Division in conjunction with their Architectural Division, establish cost budgets at a very early stage in the Design process. This enables Design and Budget to be considered in parallel in any decision making.

Quantity surveyors provide cost management and cost control skills to the design team. In a partnership approach, this enables the design team to produce the most suitable building in meeting the client's brief and staying within budget.

Additional fees for this cost consultant are much less significant than the savings that can be achieved by utilising this skill and expertise in controlling costs at all stages of construction.

Services Pre Contract

  • Early Cost advice on alternative designs and construction techniques
  • Prepare cost plans, bills of quantities and other tender documents
  • Advice on tendering and contract procedures
  • Obtain main and nominated subcontractor tenders

Services Post Contract

  • Prepare work in progress valuations and recommend payments
  • Prepare cost reports
  • Advise on financial and contractual aspects of contractors claims
  • Prepare and agree final accounts

Additional Services

  • Insurance - advice on requirements and preparation of claims
  • Developer budgets
  • Cost in use studies
  • Value engineering
  • Risk management
  • Health & Safety advice
